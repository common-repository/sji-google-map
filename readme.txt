== Installation ==

1. Upload the `sji-googlemap` plugin to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Configure your desired settings via the `Google Map Settings` settings page.
1. Done!
